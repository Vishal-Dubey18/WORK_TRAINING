Doraemon

Doraemon is a robotic cat from the future who travels back in time to help Nobita. He is known for his blue color, round shape, and a pocket from which he can pull out various futuristic gadgets to assist Nobita in his daily life.

Favorite Hobby: Eating dorayaki

Dream: To help Nobita become a better person and live a happy life.

Nobita Nobi

Nobita is a kind-hearted but lazy and clumsy boy who often struggles with his studies and sports. He frequently finds himself in trouble, but his good nature and determination to improve make him a relatable character.

Favorite Hobby: Playing video games

Dream: To become a better student and impress Shizuka.

Shizuka Minamoto

Shizuka is Nobita's kind and intelligent classmate, who is often the object of his affection. She is gentle, caring, and excels in her studies, making her a positive influence in Nobita's life.

Favorite Hobby: Playing the piano

Dream: To become a famous musician.

Gian (Takeshi Gouda)

Gian is Nobita's bully and a self-proclaimed "strongest kid" in the neighborhood. He is known for his loud voice and love of singing, but he has a softer side and cares for his friends.

Favorite Hobby: Singing

Dream: To become a famous singer.

Suneo Honekawa

Suneo is Gian's sidekick and often joins him in teasing Nobita. He is clever and enjoys showing off his wealth, but he also has moments of vulnerability.

Favorite Hobby: Collecting toys

Dream: To be the coolest kid in school.

Dorami

Dorami is Doraemon's younger sister. She is a yellow robotic cat known for her kind and caring nature. She often helps her brother and friends with her own gadgets.

Favorite Hobby: Helping others with gadgets

Dream: To become a great inventor like her brother.
